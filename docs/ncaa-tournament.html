6.1 Data Preparation

When looking at predictions of the model, they seemed to vary depending on the labeling of the teams as Team 1 and Team 2. In order to decrease this variability, I added an additional row per game with the opposite labeling of Team 1 and Team 2. The model can now view the same game result with Team 1 winning and Team 2 winning, hopefully decreasing the volatility due to randomness of labeling the teams. Once this data change was made, the modelâ€™s Log Loss for the 2021 tournament decreased to about .59, which would have placed top 50 in that competition.
